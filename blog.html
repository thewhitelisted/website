I decided to take some time to assess myself in the past year and reevaluate the goals and priorities in my life. Looking back at the start of the year, I was focused on having fun. There's nothing wrong with that, but don't be upset if you don't improve or change. I realized that I want to change. I want to continuously get better and become the best at what I do.

What is it that I want to do?
I spent some time defining some goals for myself. Making things more defined for myself meant that I could actually start working towards them. I figured out what I want to do with my career. I figured out who I want to spend my time with. I figured out how I want to approach academics.

What does it mean to be me?
I am not anyone else, nor do I want to be anyone else. I am in a constant state of change. I do not wish to be the same person I was yesterday, nor do I wish to be the same person tomorrow. I am a mathematician, someone who uses math to explain the world. I am a thinker, someone who questions everything. I am a dreamer, someone who wishes for more than what he has, and aims to achieve it through hard work.

What will I do going into year 2?
I am going to put a large focus on my academics, I wasn't the happiest with my grades this past year, and I want to change that. The side projects are going to go on hold for now.

-C
