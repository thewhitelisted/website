Risk Assessment in Financial Markets: Monte Carlo and Heston Model Simulations in Rust

A Rust implementation of Monte Carlo and Heston stochastic volatility models for simulating stock price paths and assessing financial risk via Value at Risk (VaR). Leverages async processing for efficiency and visualizes results.


I did most of the research in late february and did the coding and project writeup in the early march
