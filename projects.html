The Financial Viscosity Indicator: A Physics Inspired Stability Metric

  • Innovative Design: Combines price displacement (distance from equilibrium) and velocity (rate of change) to proactively gauge market instability, drawing analogies from viscous fluid dynamics and spring systems.
  • Empirical Promise: Demonstrated responsiveness during major market disruptions (e.g., COVID-19 crash, 2022 bear market), suggesting potential as an early-warning tool for regime shifts.
  • Work in Progress: Highlights limitations (directional agnosticism, heuristic parameters) and outlines future steps (ML integration, first-principle derivation) to evolve into a robust risk-management tool.
